# Photo credits — Big Tree BBQ website

The photographs on this site are **representative barbecue imagery, not photos of
Big Tree BBQ itself**. They are stand-ins until the Harris family supplies their
own photography of the yard, the tree, the pit and their plates (see the client
to-do list in `README.md`).

All photos are real photographs by the credited photographers, licensed
**[CC BY 2.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/)**, sourced via the
[Open Images Dataset](https://storage.googleapis.com/openimages/web/index.html)
(which indexes Flickr photos with their licenses and authorship). Images were
cropped, resized and compressed for the web; no other alterations.
