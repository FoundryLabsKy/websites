  • Major leaks — ceilings, walls or floors getting wet
  • Sewage backup — drains or toilets overflowing with waste
  • No water at all — home or business completely cut off
  • Water heater failure or leak — especially if the tank is leaking
  • Blocked main drain — nothing in the building draining

While you wait

Do this first

Shut off the water

Close the valve behind the fixture, or your main shut-off valve — usually near the water meter outside. Righty-tighty.

Kill power near the water

If water is near outlets or appliances, switch off those circuits at the breaker panel. Don't stand in water to do it.

Move what you can

Lift furniture, electronics and valuables clear. Towels at doorways slow the spread.
