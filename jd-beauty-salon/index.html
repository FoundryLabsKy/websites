About us

A little salon with a big welcome.

JD Beauty Salon and Barbers sits in the Bush Centre on North Church Street, right where George Town meets the sea. Step inside and you'll find a calm, friendly space where the trade winds of the waterfront give way to the hum of clippers, the scent of good products and easy conversation.

We're a full-service salon and barbershop in one — and that's deliberate. Couples come in together, one for a facial and one for a fade. Families book the whole morning. Regulars drop by on lunch breaks, and cruise visitors wander up from the port and leave feeling like regulars too.

Our team covers the lot: hair styling and treatments, facials, brows, threading, waxing, lashes, nails, massage and proper men's barbering. Whatever the service, the standard is the same — unhurried, precise and finished only when you're happy with the mirror.

Whether you've lived on island all your life or you're here for exactly one afternoon, you'll get the same thing from us: honest prices, careful work and a warm Caymanian welcome.
