About us

Everyday heroes for Cayman homes

No capes, no drama — just skilled hands, straight talk, and a van full of tools that shows up when we say it will.

Our story

Built on a simple frustration

Everyone in Cayman knows the feeling: a door that won't close, a deck board gone soft, an AC limping through summer — and weeks of waiting for someone to call you back. Handy Heroes started because we believed getting things fixed here shouldn't be the hardest part of the job.

So we run things differently. We answer the phone. We show up when we said we would. We quote in plain KYD before we start, do the work properly, and leave your place cleaner than we found it. Small jobs get the same respect as big ones — because to you, there's no such thing as a small problem in your own home.

We're proudly local to Grand Cayman and we work everywhere on the island, for homeowners, renters, Airbnb hosts and property managers alike.

How a job works

Four steps, zero hassle

  • 1. Reach out. Call, WhatsApp, or send the quote form — a photo of the job helps.
  • 2. Get your quote. A clear price in KYD, usually the same day. You approve before we start.
  • 3. We do the work. On time, done right, with updates if anything unexpected turns up.
  • 4. We tidy up. Mess gone, tools packed, and a quick walkthrough so you're happy before we leave.
