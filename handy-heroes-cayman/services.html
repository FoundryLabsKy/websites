Our services

Whatever the job, we've got the tools — and the manners

We respond quickly and take on jobs of any size, from a one-hour fix to a multi-week project. Every job starts with a free, no-pressure quote in KYD.

Everything we do

General Repairs

The catch-all for everything on your list: sticking doors, dripping taps, loose hinges, torn screens, wobbly ceiling fans, cracked tiles, curtain rails, shelf mounting, lock swaps. Save up the small stuff — we'll knock it all out in one visit.

AC Repair

When the AC quits in Cayman, it's an emergency — we treat it like one. Diagnostics, repairs, filter and coil cleaning, and honest advice on whether a unit is worth fixing. We'll have you cool again fast.

Painting

Interior and exterior painting done properly: surfaces prepped, cracks filled, edges taped, and salt-air-resistant paints where the weather demands it. One accent wall or the whole house — crisp lines either way.

Furniture Assembly

Beds, wardrobes, desks, bookcases, cribs, patio sets — assembled square and solid, anchored to the wall where safety calls for it, and all the packaging taken away. Perfect for new arrivals and rental turnovers.

Gutter Cleaning

Blocked gutters plus a Cayman downpour equals water where you really don't want it. We clear debris, flush the run, check downspouts and brackets, and let you know if anything needs attention before storm season.

Deck Building & Repair

New decks and pergolas designed for island sun, salt and storms — and repairs for the ones already fighting them. Board replacement, re-staining, railing fixes, structural checks. Built to be barefoot-friendly for years.

Home Improvements

The upgrades that make a place feel yours: new fixtures and fittings, storage built-ins, backsplashes, weatherproofing, fence and gate work, and those "while you're here…" projects. Tell us the vision; we'll quote it straight.

Airbnb & Rental Support

Hosts and property managers: we handle guest-turnover fixes, punch lists, and urgent call-outs so your listing never misses a booking. Photos on completion, invoices in KYD, no chasing.
