Our story

The yard was always the pride of the house

Before resorts and roundabouts, Cayman kept its beauty at home — in the yard.

Pink shells, white sand, and a standard worth keeping

Ask anyone who grew up here: the classic Caymanian yard was a thing of beauty. Mango, breadfruit and tamarind trees for shade and fruit. Hibiscus, crotons and periwinkle for colour. Paths of white sand raked smooth, bordered with pink conch shells placed one by one, and grass kept short and even all the way to the road.

It wasn't hired out. It was swept, trimmed and tended by the family — because a well-kept yard said something about the people who lived there.

Professional Mowing Service exists to keep that standard alive. We bring modern equipment, proper irrigation and premium zoysia sod to the job, but the goal hasn't changed since our grandparents' time: grounds that are the delight of the owner and the envy of the neighbours.

Today we care for family homes, business frontages, strata and beachfront properties across Grand Cayman — every one of them treated like our own yard.
