Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need to book a table?

There's no formal booking — it's counter service at the window with picnic-table seating. For groups, or to make sure your dish is still on, it's best to call ahead on +1 345-947-7435.

Can I pay by card?

Vivine's is cash only. There's no ATM nearby out east, so bring enough cash before you make the drive. Both KYD and USD are commonly used in Cayman.

What's actually on the menu today?

It changes daily and by season — the menu is chalked on a blackboard each morning based on what's fresh. Turtle, conch, lobster and sorrel are seasonal. The only way to know for sure is to call.

Is there anything for non-seafood eaters?

Yes — curried goat, curry and stew chicken, oxtail, BBQ chicken and ribs are regulars, alongside rice & beans, cassava, plantain and other sides.

Do you serve alcohol?

No alcohol is served. Instead there's fresh, cold local juice — tamarind, mango and sorrel when it's in season.

How do I get there from George Town or Seven Mile Beach?

Head east along the coast toward East End and keep going to Gun Bay — roughly a 40–50 minute drive. Use the directions link and give us a call if you get turned around.
